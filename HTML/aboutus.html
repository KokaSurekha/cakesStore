Founder

Riaz-ul-Hassan is the founder of Cakes & Bakes, a bakery chain that started in 1954 as Ajmair Confectioners in Gulberg Lahore. He took over operations and transformed it into an expanding bakery chain. Muhammad Usman is the owner of Cakes & Bakes.Riaz Ul Hassan, the current Chief Executive took over the operations, he granted a new vision and transformed it from a conventional bakery to an expanding bakery chain, Cakes & Bakes.
